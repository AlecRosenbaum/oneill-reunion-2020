Accomodations

We will be staying at this lovely house on Lake Harmony! It has 14 bedrooms and 12 baths and sleeps 42 so there should be more than enough room for all of us! There are two full kitchens, three decks, and two small docks!

The ten single bedrooms will be offered to the parental units first and any leftovers will be on a first come first serve basis. The remaining four bedrooms have four bunk beds, we can set up a chart for accomodations.

Because it is such a large house, they ask that we provide our own linens, so please bring a set of sheets and towels :)
